NBA teams and referees perfromance in last two minutes

NBA publishes reviews of officiated events that occurred in the last 120 seconds of games which were within five points at the two minute mark. The data includes all calls (whistles) and notable non-calls. Now we can find out which team has the steadiest pair of hands in the nerve wracking endgame.

Fans know it best when their team is not performing as expected. Complaining about players, the coaching staff but most often the referees are the source of their favourite teams under performance. Now we can explore how many incorrect calls and non-calls are made in favour and against each team.

The chart visualises the team statistic for incorrect calls in favour and against. The timeline shows in 5 seconds intervals when and how many of them occur in the last 120 seconds of the game.
