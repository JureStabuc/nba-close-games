NBA publishes reviews of officiated events that occurred in the last 120 seconds of games which were within five points at the two minute mark. The data includes all calls (whistles) and notable non-calls. We can find out which team has the steadiest hand and calmest nerves.

More than often fans complain when their team is not performing as expected. Mostly it is about referees and how they help the opponent. But although some teams perform better in close games it does not necessarily mean that they had some help from the officials in the last seconds.

The chart visualises the team statistic for incorrect calls, how many were in favour and against. The timeline show when and how many of them occur in the last 120 seconds of the game in 5 seconds intervals.
